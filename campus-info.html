Pilestredet Campus

Oslomets' largest campus is located in the heart of Oslo. It is the most urban university campus of Oslo. We offer a big variety of studies here, from nursing to IT-studies. The campus consists of many buildings, where different type of studies belongs to different buildings. Pilestredet campus is compact and everything is within walking distance. Address: Pilestredet 32-54.

Photo: Oslomet.no

Cafeteria Oslo campus

There are several cantines in the Oslo campus. We have both prepared warm food, beverages, coffee and a lot more. With a lively atmosphere, it's the perfect place to recharge between classes. Whether you're craving a hot meal, a refreshing drink, or just a cozy place to chat with friends, the student canteen is the go-to spot for good eats and good times on campus! Read more here and find your faculties' closest cantine.

Student unions

There are several student unions to join at Oslomet. If you like gaming, knitting, sports or any other activity - you are guaranteed to find a student union for you. Student unions is a great way to meet other students and connect through hobbies that you have in common.

Photo: Freepik.com

Datatorget

If you are a computer science student, you can sit at Datatorget and work with your co-students. This is a space for students to be social, work in groups and discuss coding.

Photo: Freepik.com

Climbing wall

Experienced or completely new to the climbing game? At Oslomet you can practice your climbing skills on our very own climbing wall at Pilestredet 35. Inside or outside, these walls make you use your muscles and brain. It's a cool way to challenge yourself and feel proud when you reach the top. Climbing walls are all about having fun and enjoying the adventure!
